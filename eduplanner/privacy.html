what information do we collect?

personal identification information

We may collect personal identification information from Users only in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our app. Users may be asked for only their email address. We will collect this personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily submit such information to us. Users can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in extra functionalities provided by the app.

The application stores user data and will only transmit non-identifying user data to a third party if the user voluntarily chooses so.

We use other technologies to keep track of your interactions with our app and services to improve the app

Eduplanner does not store Users' personal information. However, the app may use Users' email address to identify Users against third-party services for added functionality, if the Users voluntarily choose to do so.
